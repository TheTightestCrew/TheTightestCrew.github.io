Crimefest is a holiday celebrating the worship of Almir, and the life of Yung Lean. It is a holiday that lasts several weeks in October. During crimefest, Almir showers his worshipers with love and DLC. Almir worshipers and Rabi of the Synagogue of DLC alike worship the god during these weeks. It is one of the most important holidays in the Church of Almir, besides the birth of Yung Lean of course. Festivities occur in major cities and where the influence of Almirism is strong. Many heathens do not know the name of Almir. Crimefest is a time to spread his word of his glory. Prominent members of the Almirism community go door to door spreading the gospel of Almir. They show the pitiful heathens and pagans that Almir is the true god. They show that Yung Lean is the true messiah.

Crimefest is much like The Sales that happen every season. During the sales Gaben speads his sales. During crimefest, Almir spreads his DLC.
